About Monkey King

Monkey King, a legendary figure with decades of experience in the investment field, is renowned for his unique value investing philosophy and exceptional market insights.

He previously held senior executive positions in major corporations, leading teams to achieve impressive investment results through his profound financial knowledge and rich practical experience.

Monkey King is not only a practitioner in the capital market but also a pioneer in theory. His book, "Value Investing," lucidly explains the core essence of value investing, becoming an essential read for countless investors.

His investment philosophy emphasizes a deep understanding of a company's intrinsic value, advocates for long-term holding, and firmly believes in finding undervalued quality assets amidst market fluctuations.

Investment Philosophy

Deep Value Analysis

Focus on understanding a company's true intrinsic value beyond market fluctuations.

Long-Term Perspective

Advocate for patient investing and holding quality assets through market cycles.

Risk Management

Prioritize capital preservation and avoid unnecessary risks.
